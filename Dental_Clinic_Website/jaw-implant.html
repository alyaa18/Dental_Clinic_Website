What are Jaw Implants?

Jaw implants are a surgical solution used to enhance the shape, structure, and balance of the jawline. They help correct facial asymmetry, restore definition lost due to trauma or aging, and improve overall facial aesthetics. This procedure can significantly boost confidence by providing a stronger, more defined jaw contour.
